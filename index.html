Quick answers to some common questions.

Are the introductions exclusive?

Yes. Every introduction is 1:1 and never shared with competing parties in your vertical during the engagement. Once we route a conversation, that room is closed to anyone else in the same category.

Is our information kept private?

Always. Nothing about your mandate, your criteria, or your conversations is shared outside the routing layer. Both sides only see what's relevant to make the room work.

How is this different from brokers or advisors?

Brokers and advisors work a deal after it exists. We work before it does — reading signal, qualifying fit, and making the introduction happen in the first place. We're not paid to advise on a transaction. We're paid to make the right one possible.

What type of outcomes do clients use this for?

New revenue from a market you couldn't access on your own, a strategic partnership that changes your roadmap, or capital from the right allocator at the right time — see the case studies above for what that's looked like in practice.
